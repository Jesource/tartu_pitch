Studying Computer Science in University of Tartu

Studying datavitenskap (computer science) at University of Tartu can be an spennende (exciting) and challenging experience. The university offers a wide range of courses and programs in datavitenskap (data science), including bachelor's and master's degrees in informatikk (informatics), programvareutvikling (software development) and more.

One of the unique features of studying datavitenskap at University of Tartu is the opportunity to be part of an international student group. The has a large number of international students from around the world, which provides a diverse and multicultural learning environment.
